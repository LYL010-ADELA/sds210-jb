## Contact

**Questions about course content or exercises**  
For questions related to the course material (e.g. *“How do I resolve problem X in task Y?”*), please use the *respective Microsoft Teams channel*. This way helps everyone benefit from the discussion.

To help us support you efficiently, always include:

* a brief description of the problem
* the exact error message (if any)
* code snippet or a screenshot
* a Colab or GitHub link (when applicable)

**Personal or administrative questions**  
For personal matters or course-related questions specific to SDS210, ESS342 or GEO876, please contact the module coordinator: [hendrik.wulf@geo.uzh.ch](mailto:hendrik.wulf@geo.uzh.ch)

Office hours (during the semester):
Tuesdays and Wednesdays, 14:00–15:00 in room Y25-K-12
